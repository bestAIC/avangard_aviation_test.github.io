Engineering
& Technical Services

Avangard Aviation is a full-service aircraft engineering and technical center. Operating under Part 145 Maintenance Organization Approval Certificate DE.145.0473 issued by Luftfahrt-Bundeasamt, the aviation licensing and operational safety oversight organization of Germany, Avangard Aviation provides the finest in German engineering with a state-of-the-art facility and an ideal location in the center of Europe

We offer the full range
of technical services including:

Base maintenance up to and including heavy checks

Line maintenance at our line maintenance stations and on-call elsewhere

AOG services including a “Go Team” ready to travel on short notice

Troubleshooting, modifications and systems upgrades

Engineering services

Factory-authorized warranty work

Spare parts inventory management, logistics, supply and exchange

Full interior and exterior cleaning, repairs and updates

Ground handling and VIP lounge at our modern hangar facility

Aircraft parking and storage, short-term and long-term at our spacious facility

Line maintenance station with personnel, hangar parts and equipment at Moscow

Our team of German engineers is capable of handling
